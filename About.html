Lil' Bitty Bishop Farm

About Us

Business history?



In 2012, the Bishop's bought an old farm house in Longview on three acres. They continued to develop the land and added farm animals. Gary and Angie took advantage of the resources the animals provided them and began selling products. They attend courses and workshops that has developed and fine-tunes the 'farm-life'. They continue to strive to add more products and welcome feedback for continuous improvements and satisfaction of products.

Product Description

Lil' Bitty Bishop Farm offers all-natural goat soap, bath bombs, perfume and lotions. Each item comes in a variety of scents of your choice. Products are continued to be developed so please return often to see what's new!
